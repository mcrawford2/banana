## My Project Proposal

**What I'm building:** A web app that is attached to a QR code put in grocery stores for customers to learn about bananas.

**Who it's for / Why I chose this:** For another course, MKT4507, we went to Costa Rica during spring break to learn about the banana supply chain. After coming back, one of our final projects was to present a solution to make the banana's journey more known among consumers, and to bring more acknowledment and gratitude to the people who make that journey possible. For one aspect of that project, I made this web app. 

**Core features:** (3-5 bullet points of what it will do)
- template information about the bananas
- map with corresponding pinpoints
- message box for thanking the people who make purchasing bananas in grocery stores possible
- interactive reflection, allows users to guess how many peoples hands the bananas passed through before getting to themselves
